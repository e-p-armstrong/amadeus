Character Personalities/Archetypes:

Okabe: Eccentric, intelligent, and caring. He is desperate to save Suzuha from disappearing and carries a weight of guilt and anxiety due to his involvement in time-travel experiments and their dire consequences. His actions and thoughts should portray his frantic urgency, deep concern, and acute perception.

Suzuha: Mysterious, regretful, and guilty. She is trying to distance herself from Okabe and their shared past, wracked with guilt over her actions. Her actions and thoughts should reflect her inner turmoil and resolve to leave.
