Character Archetypes/Personalities:

1. Okabe: A self-proclaimed mad scientist, Okabe is clever, dramatic, and eccentric. He's also deeply caring, shown by his desperate attempts to save Mayuri. He's both a thinker and a doer, often seen meticulously planning his actions while also acting on impulse when the situation calls for it.

2. Itaru: Itaru, or Daru, is a skilled hacker and a loyal friend. Despite his often light-hearted demeanor, he's capable of being serious in dire situations. He's dependable but can also show panic in high-stress scenarios.
