Character Archetypes/Personalities: 

Okabe: Okabe is eccentric, prone to dramatic outbursts and monologues, but also deeply caring about his friends. He's been burdened with the task of changing the past to save his friends, a responsibility that weighs heavily on him. His actions and thoughts should reflect his dramatic nature, as well as his internal struggle and guilt.

Kurisu: Kurisu is intelligent, rational, and somewhat reserved. She is often the voice of reason in the group, and despite her initial skepticism, she eventually becomes deeply involved in their time-traveling experiments. Her actions and thoughts should reflect her logical, analytical nature, as well as her growing concern for Okabe and the others.
