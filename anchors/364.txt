- Okabe: Eccentric, intelligent, and driven by a sense of responsibility, Okabe is a self-proclaimed mad scientist who often masks his deeper, more sensitive side with a flamboyant persona. - Kurisu: Highly intelligent, pragmatic, and caring, Kurisu is a genius scientist who often plays the rational counterpoint to Okabe's eccentricity. Her actions and thoughts will be marked by her analytical approach to situations and her concealed concern for Okabe and his dilemma.
- Luka: Gentle, shy, and deeply sensitive, Luka is Okabe's friend who is unknowingly at the center of this ethical quandary. Her reactions and emotions will be portrayed with a sense of innocence and vulnerability.
