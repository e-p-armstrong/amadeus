Planning:

1. Characters:
   - Okabe: He is eccentric, dramatic, and flamboyant. His actions should reflect his larger-than-life personality, with grand gestures and theatrical language in his thoughts.
   - Mayuri: She is sweet, innocent, and a bit childish. Her actions should be simple and endearing, reflective of her pure and positive nature.
   - Nakabachi: He is arrogant and confident. His actions should be commanding and his thoughts should reflect his self-assured nature.
   
2. Word Choices/Writing Styles:
   - Okabe's actions and thoughts will be written in a flamboyant and dramatic style. I'll use strong, powerful words to capture his intensity and eccentricity.
   - Mayuri's actions and thoughts will be written in a gentle and innocent style. I'll use soft, warm words to convey her simple happiness and childlike fascination.
   - Nakabachi's actions and thoughts will be written in a commanding and confident style. I'll use assertive, bold words to depict his arrogance and overconfidence.

3. Anchor Lines: Okabe's confrontation with Nakabachi. I will enhance this moment to underline Okabe's theatrical nature and his determination to expose Nakabachi's fraud.
