Character Archetypes/Personalities:

1. Okabe: Eccentric, dramatic, and passionate. He likes to role-play as a mad scientist and uses a lot of dramatic language. He's also quite intelligent and cares deeply about his friends.

2. Suzuha: Strong, straightforward, and loyal. She's a time-traveler who's willing to fight for what she believes in. She has a sense of humor and can be a bit blunt.

3. Tennouji: Gruff, pragmatic, and a little bit grouchy. He's the owner of the store Suzuha works at and doesn't have much patience for Okabe's antics.

4. Mayuri: Sweet, innocent, and a little bit naive. She's also a bit of a foodie and isn't easily intimidated.

Word Choices/Writing Styles:

1. Okabe: Dramatic language, lots of movement and grand gestures. Thoughts are complex and sometimes a bit paranoid.

2. Suzuha: Straightforward language, strong and decisive actions. Thoughts are practical and focused on the task at hand.

3. Tennouji: Gruff language, impatient actions. Thoughts are pragmatic and focused on his store.

4. Mayuri: Sweet and innocent language, gentle and cheerful actions. Thoughts are simple and often focused on food.
