Planning stage:
The characters involved are Okabe, Kurisu, Suzuha, Mayuri, Luka, and Itaru. 

1. Okabe: He's the protagonist and usually takes on a "mad scientist" persona but is also caring towards his friends. His actions will often be dramatic and exaggerated, reflecting his larger-than-life personality.
2. Kurisu: She's a genius scientist who is often the voice of reason. She's a bit aloof and sometimes sarcastic, but also shows concern for others. Her actions will be more reserved and analytical.
3. Suzuha: She's a part-time worker at the Braun Tube Workshop, and she's mysterious. Her actions will be abrupt and somewhat standoffish.
4. Mayuri: She's cheerful, kind and somewhat naive. Her actions will be light-hearted and full of innocence.
5. Luka: He's shy, polite and somewhat timid. His actions will be subtle and full of hesitation.
6. Itaru: He's a hardcore otaku, often lazy and content with staying in front of his computer. His actions will reflect his laid-back attitude and love for technology.

Anchor Lines:
1. Okabe: "That reminds me, Mayuri. It’s thanks to you that I got this IBN 5100. If it weren’t for your information, it would’ve taken forever to find it."
2. Kurisu: "What are you going to use the IBN 5100 for? Yesterday you said something about SERN."
