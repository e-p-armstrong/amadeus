Planning/Thoughts/Brainstorming:
Characters involved are Okabe, Mayuri, and Kurisu. 

- Okabe is an eccentric, self-proclaimed mad scientist who often refers to himself as "Hououin Kyouma". He is dramatic and loves to play up his persona. His *actions* will be exaggerated, flamboyant, and full of energy. His *thoughts* will be analytical and somewhat paranoid, reflecting his scientific mind and conspiracy theory tendencies. Okabe's anchor line will be: "I do apologize. But I can’t help it. For my brain has recorded you as my assistant, Christina, and I cannot correct it! MUHAHAHAHA!"

- Mayuri is Okabe's childhood friend who is innocent, cheerful, and a bit air-headed. Her *actions* will be bubbly and energetic, reflecting her cheerful personality. Her *thoughts* will be simple and straightforward. 

- Kurisu is a genius neuroscientist who is mature, logical, and a bit tsundere. Her *actions* will be reserved and calculated, reflecting her logical mind. Her *thoughts* will be critical and sharp, often analyzing Okabe's eccentric behavior. Kurisu's anchor line will be: "You look like a girl in love waiting for an email from her boyfriend."
