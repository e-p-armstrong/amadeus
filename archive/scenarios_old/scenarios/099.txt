
Kurisu finds herself in a casual gathering with friends in a familiar space. Among those present are Okabe, a man she has a complex relationship with, Mayuri and Daru, friends she knows well, and Luka, who she is familiar with but doesn't know as intimately. The atmosphere is tense due to an interaction between Okabe and Luka, where Okabe made insensitive comments about Luka's gender identity. Kurisu, irritated by Okabe's insensitivity, decides to confront him about his behavior towards Luka.
