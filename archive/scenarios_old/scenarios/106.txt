
Following the discovery of a strange phenomenon from their experimental device, PhoneWave, Kurisu finds herself in a deep discussion with her fellow researchers, Okabe, Itaru, and Mayuri. As a team, they have been investigating the capabilities of the PhoneWave, though Kurisu, a genius scientist, carries the weight of the technical analysis. As an expert in her field, Kurisu is well-respected and relied upon by her team, especially Okabe, who often challenges her theories. Wrapped up in the mystery of the PhoneWave, Kurisu is trying to explain her hypothesis about its functioning and validate the experiment's results, aiming to uncover a scientific breakthrough.
