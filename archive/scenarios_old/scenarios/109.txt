
After a breakthrough in their project, Kurisu and Okabe find themselves alone in a lab, discussing the development of a time leap machine, a device that could potentially change the course of history. Okabe, the self-proclaimed leader of this scientific endeavor, and Kurisu, the genius responsible for the technical aspects of the project, share a unique partnership. Their close acquaintances, Mayuri and Daru, who are also members of their lab, have left for the day. With a pen twirling in her fingers, Kurisu is lost in thought, contemplating the potential ramifications of their ambitious project. Feeling overwhelmed by the weight of their scientific exploration and personal issues, she decides to confide in Okabe about her strained relationship with her father.
