
Kurisu is in a laboratory with Okabe and their friends, engrossed in a troubling news broadcast about a bomb threat in Akihabara. Having worked together on a scientific project, Kurisu knows everyone present quite well, including Suzuha who suddenly displays unusual behavior. The atmosphere is tense and Kurisu is confused as Suzuha abruptly leaves after inquiring about the status of their Time Leap Machine. Trying to make sense of it all, Kurisu is determined to understand the connection between Suzuha's strange behavior and the ongoing crisis.
