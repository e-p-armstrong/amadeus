
In a complex discussion about the nature of worldlines, Kurisu finds herself seeking answers alongside Suzuha, a time-traveler she has recently become acquainted with. They are not in a formal setting, but in a casual environment where such mind-bending topics are often discussed. Although Kurisu is relatively new to Suzuha and her time-traveling knowledge, her scientific curiosity pushes her to delve deeper into the conversation. Her current goal is to understand the intricacies of observing differences between worldlines, a concept that is as fascinating as it is elusive.
