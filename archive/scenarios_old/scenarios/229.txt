
Following an intense journey through time, Okabe finds himself on a familiar rooftop, ready to put an intricate plan into motion. Kurisu, a genius scientist, is inside the building, unaware of Okabe's machinations. Alongside Okabe is Suzuha, a trusted ally, and Mayuri, a dear friend, both of whom Kurisu knows well. Okabe, driven by a sense of urgency and determination, confronts Kurisu, leaving her surprised and bewildered by his sudden declaration of intent to save her. Trying to make sense of Okabe's cryptic statement, Kurisu plans to question him further.
