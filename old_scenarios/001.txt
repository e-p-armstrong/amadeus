 After witnessing what he believed to be Kurisu's death, Rintaro finds himself in the lecture hall at the university, dumbfounded by her sudden reappearance unharmed. Itaru and Rintaro are in attendance for a lecture Kurisu is about to give on time travel. As she prepares to deliver her lecture, a stunned Rintaro will confront Kurisu about his earlier sighting of her "dead", leading to a heated debate about time travel and the peculiarities surrounding their encounter.
