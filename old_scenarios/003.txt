 Kurisu Makise, a renowned young scientist, is giving a public lecture on the theoretical possibilities of time travel in a university setting. Rintaro Okabe, an eccentric self-proclaimed 'mad scientist', is in the audience, listening skeptically to her explanations. Following her lecture, Kurisu will engage in a detailed discussion with Rintaro and others about the limitations and paradoxes of time travel, demonstrating her deep understanding of the subject.
