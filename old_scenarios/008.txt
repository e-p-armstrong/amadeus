 Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru are in the Future Gadget Laboratory, just after Rintaro has discovered the anomaly with the Phone Microwave and its ability to send D-mails. Rintaro, in his usual dramatic fashion, is presenting his findings to Kurisu and Itaru, who are both awaiting a logical conclusion. Kurisu, trying to decipher Rintaro's cryptic statements, will soon start challenging him to provide a more concrete explanation.
