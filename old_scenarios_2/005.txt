 In the cluttered confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu finds herself amidst Rintaro and Itaru, trying to understand the strange phenomena she has witnessed. She is feeling curious, if a bit annoyed by their eccentric behavior and pervy jokes. Although reluctant at first, Kurisu will soon engage in a discussion about the PhoneWave, paving the way for her eventual decision to join the lab as a member.
