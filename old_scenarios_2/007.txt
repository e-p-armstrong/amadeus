 In a surprising twist of events, Kurisu is alive and well after Rintaro had found her in a pool of blood. At the Future Gadget Laboratory, the group is discussing the strange circumstances surrounding Dr. Nakabachi's cancelled presentation and the mysterious email Rintaro sent about Kurisu's supposed death. Confronting Rintaro about these inconsistencies, Kurisu is about to question him further, seeking clarification about the events that had led to her 'death'.
