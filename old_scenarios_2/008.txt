 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is in the middle of a revelation about the anomalous behavior of the Phone Microwave, while Kurisu and Itaru listen. The atmosphere is tense with Rintaro's excitement and Itaru and Kurisu waiting for him to make sense of his discovery. Puzzled and a bit irritated, Kurisu will attempt to get Rintaro to explain his dramatic realization in more practical scientific terms.
