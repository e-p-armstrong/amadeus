 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are struggling to move the recently obtained IBN 5100, a crucial piece of equipment for their time travel research. Kurisu is frustrated with Rintaro's antics but is also showing concern for his health, indicating their relationship has deepened. With the arrival of the IBN 5100, Kurisu will now help in deciphering its mysterious code to aid their time travel experiments.
