 After Rintaro's successful acquisition of the IBN 5100, the group is in the process of moving it to the Future Gadget Laboratory. Kurisu is taken aback by a sudden, hostile encounter with Suzuha from the Braun Tube Workshop, leaving her feeling puzzled and a little hurt. As they continue moving the IBN 5100, Kurisu will try to brush off the strange interaction, and focus on helping Rintaro despite her confusion and fatigue.
