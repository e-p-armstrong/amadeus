 Inside the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, Mayuri, and Itaru gather after another day of experimentation with the PhoneWave (name subject to change). As Kurisu becomes more involved with the lab and its projects, she starts to question their knowledge about SERN and the mysterious IBN 5100. Feeling curious and determined, Kurisu will press Rintaro and Itaru for answers about their unexpected discoveries and the dangerous implications they carry.
