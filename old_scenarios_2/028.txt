 Within the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are delving into the secrets of SERN's Z Program after realizing the potential dangers of their own time travel experiments. Kurisu, feeling both anxious and determined, is methodically explaining the stages of the Z Program, revealing the potential for human experimentation. Despite the tension, she will continue to shed light on SERN's plans, hoping to understand more about the consequences they might face if they continue their own experiments.
