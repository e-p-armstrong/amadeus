 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru are about to uncover the dark truth about SERN's time travel experiments through the Jellyman's Report. The atmosphere is tense, with Rintaro fearing for Kurisu's safety due to the dangerous implications of their discovery. Despite Rintaro's concern, Kurisu remains resolute, determined to expose the truth of SERN's unethical experiments to the world.
