 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Itaru, Mayuri, and newly arrived Moeka are collaborating on the development of a new version of the PhoneWave, while simultaneously engaging in their usual hijinks. Kurisu, despite participating in the conversation, is growing increasingly frustrated with the team's lack of focus and their propensity for nonsensical banter. In the upcoming moments, the ever-pragmatic Kurisu will attempt to steer the team's attention back to the task at hand, despite the distractions.
