 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro and the others have begun to understand the potential of D-mails and are pondering the possibilities of sending information back in time. In this friendly and relaxed atmosphere, Kurisu, despite her usually serious demeanor, is engaging in a playful, hypothetical discussion with Mayuri about the implications of time-altered lottery winnings. In the process, Kurisu will gently tease Mayuri about her innocence and lack of worldly knowledge.
