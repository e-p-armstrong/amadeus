 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro has just introduced the concept of "Steins Gate," a term he uses to describe a specific timeline. Kurisu, still new to the complex and abstract ideas Rintaro frequently talks about, is trying to understand these concepts. Frustrated by Rintaro's cryptic explanation, Kurisu will push for more clarity, trying to unravel the mystery behind the term "Steins Gate".
