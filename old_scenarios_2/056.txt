 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is eager to proceed with Operation Urd, the series of experiments involving the sending of D-Mails to change the past, despite Kurisu's ethical reservations. The team is still in the phase of exploration and discovery, with the negative consequences of their actions yet to materialize. A conflicted Kurisu, struggling with the moral implications of their activities, will express her disapproval and attempt to distance herself from the experiment.
