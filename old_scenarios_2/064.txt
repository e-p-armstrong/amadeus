 It's an unusual evening at the Future Gadget Laboratory; Luka is present, dressed in a costume that the group is admiring, and Rintaro is ready to grant Luka's deepest wish using their recently discovered D-mail ability. Kurisu, though initially flustered, is intrigued by the new cultural experiences, finding them both amusing and confusing. Starting from a place of wary curiosity, Kurisu will soon find herself drawn into the group's plans to use the D-mails, not realizing the consequences that will follow.
