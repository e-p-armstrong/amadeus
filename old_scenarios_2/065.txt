 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, the lab members, including Kurisu, are engaging in a lighthearted discussion about their individual wishes, highlighting their camaraderie and the relative peace of this period in their lives. Unsettled by this revelation and Rintaro's teasing, Kurisu will try to recover her composure, while also making sure Itaru doesn't take advantage of the situation.
