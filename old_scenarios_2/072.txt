 Amid the summer heat, the Future Gadget Laboratory team is heavily engrossed in their ongoing time travel experiments, with Kurisu reluctantly forgoing a vacation to contribute her expertise. Despite her complaints about the lab conditions and her colleagues, Kurisu's commitment to the project is evident, and she maintains a friendly, albeit sarcastic, rapport with the group. The conversation is poised to involve heated debates about the potential and dangers of physical time travel, with Kurisu bringing a skeptical and scientific perspective to the table.
