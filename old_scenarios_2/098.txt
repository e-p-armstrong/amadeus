 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, after the development of the Time Leap machine, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru, along with Mayuri, are deep in a discussion about the implications and ethics of their new invention. Kurisu, feeling a mix of accomplishment and worry, is trying to ensure everyone understands the risks involved with using the machine. In the upcoming conversation, Kurisu will continue to explain the intricacies and potential dangers of time leaping, in an attempt to make an informed decision about who should be the first test subject.
