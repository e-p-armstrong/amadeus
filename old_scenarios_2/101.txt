 In the midst of a sudden and terrifying SERN raid on the Future Gadget Laboratory, tragedy strikes as Mayuri is killed. A panicked and desperate Kurisu, terrified for her friends' lives, tries to calm a furious and distraught Rintaro, urging him not to resist their attackers. Kurisu, torn between fear and determination, will attempt to negotiate with the SERN agents to spare Rintaro's life, while grappling with the harsh reality of their situation.
