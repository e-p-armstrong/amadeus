 In a tense moment at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and the others are under attack from SERN, who has discovered their time travel experiments. Despite the chaos and the immediate threat to their lives, Rintaro is determined to use the Time Leap machine to save Mayuri, while Kurisu is arguing against it out of fear of failure or further complications. Amidst the fear and desperation, Kurisu is about to make a crucial decision, either trying to stop Rintaro from his reckless endeavor or supporting him in his desperate attempt to change the timeline.
