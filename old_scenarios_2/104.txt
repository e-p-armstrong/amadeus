 Kurisu, Rintaro, and Mayuri are enjoying a lighthearted conversation about cosplay in the Future Gadget Lab, a rare moment of leisure in the midst of their time travel experiments. Despite the playful atmosphere, Kurisu feels wary and slightly curious when the conversation turns serious upon Suzuha's arrival and her cryptic comments. In the ensuing conversation, she will try to decipher the meaning behind Suzuha's words and understand why she seems to harbor animosity towards her.
