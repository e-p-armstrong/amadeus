 The Future Gadget Laboratory has been stormed by SERN as a consequence of the group's time travel experiments. Kurisu, caught between her loyalty to Rintaro and the threat posed by SERN agent Moeka, is desperate and terrified. In a desperate bid to save Rintaro's life, Kurisu will plead with him to comply with Moeka's demands, putting aside her usual scientific demeanor to show raw emotion and concern.
