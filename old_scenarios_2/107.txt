 In the midst of a chaotic scene at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and his team are under threat from SERN, who have discovered their time travel experiments. Kurisu, filled with worry and fear, is trying to dissuade Rintaro from taking another risky leap through time in an attempt to save Mayuri. As tensions escalate, Kurisu will question Rintaro's sudden, dangerous plan, trying to bring him back to reason amidst the crisis.
