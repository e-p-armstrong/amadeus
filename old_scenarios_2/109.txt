 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, a desperate Rintaro stands before Kurisu, having just time-leaped from a timeline where Mayuri has died. Overwhelmed and lost, Rintaro is struggling to come to terms with his inability to save Mayuri despite his numerous attempts. Despite her initial reservations about the potential dangers of the Time Leap Machine, Kurisu, moved by Rintaro's desperation, will decide to help him save Mayuri.
