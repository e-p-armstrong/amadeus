 After multiple failed attempts to save Mayuri's life, Rintaro and Kurisu are in the Future Gadget Laboratory, preparing to use the Time Leap Machine once again. Kurisu, feeling both empathetic towards Rintaro's relentless struggles and determined to prevent Mayuri's death, devises a new strategy for Rintaro's next time leap. She will explain her plan in detail, firmly emphasizing the importance of both preserving the Time Leap Machine and gaining her own trust in the past.
