 Rintaro and Kurisu are in the Future Gadget Laboratory, in the midst of a crisis following the repeated deaths of Mayuri in various timelines. Rintaro is desperate to find a way to save Mayuri, while Kurisu is trying to keep him grounded in the realities and limitations of their time travel technology. With Rintaro on the edge, Kurisu is set to explain the theoretical boundaries of their time leap machine and brainstorm possible solutions for their predicament.
