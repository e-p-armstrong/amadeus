 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are grappling with the limitations of their newly discovered Time Leap Machine, a device that can send memories to the past. They are in the middle of a heated discussion about the potential dangers of SERN, who they fear might kill their friend Mayuri in a matter of hours. Kurisu, feeling a mix of frustration and concern, will attempt to temper Rintaro's desperation and impulsive nature, reminding him of the risks involved.
