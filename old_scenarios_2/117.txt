 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, after learning about Suzuha's true identity as a time-traveler and her mission, Kurisu and Rintaro are now being introduced to the concept of Attractor Fields and world lines - key elements in understanding the fabric of time and space. The atmosphere is tense and filled with a sense of urgency as they grapple with the grim reality of their situation and the complexity of time travel. Kurisu, in her relentless pursuit of knowledge, will question Suzuha further, hoping to fully grasp this new paradigm and its implications for their ongoing struggle against the dystopian future.
