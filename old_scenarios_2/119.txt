 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are in the midst of a tense discussion after Suzuha has revealed her identity as a time-traveller from the year 2036. Kurisu, analytical and skeptical as always, is questioning the validity of Suzuha's claims and her extensive knowledge about them. Despite her initial skepticism, Kurisu will strive to understand and validate Suzuha's information, as she grapples with the reality of time-travel and its implications.
