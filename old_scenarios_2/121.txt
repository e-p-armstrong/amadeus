 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Itaru, Mayuri, Suzuha, and Kurisu are trying to resolve an issue with the Time Leap Machine. Despite the group's optimistic and lighthearted banter, Kurisu is silent, deep in thought, grappling with the enormity of their situation and the technical difficulties they face. Her silence indicates that she will soon take the lead in troubleshooting the problem, relying on her expertise to guide them towards a solution.
