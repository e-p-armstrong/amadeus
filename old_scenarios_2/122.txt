 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Suzuha are discussing their next steps after the shocking revelation of Suzuha's true identity as John Titor, the time traveler. The atmosphere is serious and tense as they are dealing with the consequences of their time-travel experiments and the impending danger from SERN. Kurisu, feeling both concerned and skeptical, will question Rintaro's plan to leap back in time and repair the time machine, attempting to ensure they're making the best possible decisions moving forward.
