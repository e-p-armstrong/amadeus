 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are grappling with the grim reality of a broken time machine and the task of locating Suzuha's father. With the strain of their mission evident, Kurisu tries to lighten the mood with her bluntness and dry humor. As they discuss their plans, Kurisu will assertively shift the focus back to the repair work, determined to make progress now that she has new information about the lifter.
