 As they complete the Time Leap Machine in the cluttered Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu and Rintaro, along with time-traveler Suzuha, find themselves intrigued and puzzled by the mechanics of time leaping and the unique phenomenon of Rintaro's Reading Steiner ability. Kurisu, a brilliant scientist driven by curiosity, is left somewhat frustrated by the unknowns surrounding the inexplicable link between memory and consciousness in relation to time travel. With more questions than answers, Kurisu will soon delve deeper into these mysteries, trying to unravel the fundamental principles that govern their newly invented time-leaping technology.
