 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Mayuri are grappling with the repercussions of their time-travel experiments, specifically the unraveling timelines and the impending doom that befalls Mayuri. Rintaro is desperate to change the outcomes while Kurisu and Mayuri watch on, anxious and uncertain. Kurisu, despite her confusion, will provide Rintaro with the crucial information he needs to attempt to rectify the timelines.
