 Kurisu, Suzuha, Itaru, and Mayuri found themselves in a sudden, unexpected situation when a reckless truck nearly ran into them while they were out in the streets of Akihabara. Shaken but unharmed, they are relieved to have escaped the dangerous situation. Despite the shock, Kurisu maintains her pragmatic nature, reminding everyone that since no harm was done, there's no need to report the driver.
