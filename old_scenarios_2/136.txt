 After a day of exploration, Kurisu, Mayuri, and Itaru find themselves in the vast, empty space usually bustling with activity during the ComiMa event. They've ventured out of the confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, taking a break from their usual experiments. Feeling the fatigue from their day-long excursion, Kurisu will soon express her annoyance at the daunting prospect of a long bike ride back to the lab.
