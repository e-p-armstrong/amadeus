 In the cluttered confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are locked in yet another one of their heated debates. Though they are both working towards the same goal of perfecting the PhoneWave, their clashing personalities often lead to fierce arguments. Taken aback by Rintaro's sudden insult, Kurisu will soon regain her composure and snap back, maintaining the spirited back-and-forth that characterizes their early interactions.
