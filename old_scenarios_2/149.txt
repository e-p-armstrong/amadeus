 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, after Rintaro has agreed to go on a date with Urushibara Luka in order to undo a D-mail, he finds himself discussing the upcoming event with Kurisu. Kurisu, hiding behind a mask of sarcasm and wit, is trying to suppress her emotions about Rintaro's date, despite her growing feelings for him. Despite her feelings, Kurisu is about to offer Rintaro dating advice, trying to help him succeed in his mission to save Mayuri.
