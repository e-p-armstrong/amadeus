 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu is acting as a mediator between the absent Mayuri and Rintaro, who has been so engrossed in his experiments with the PhoneWave that he has neglected his personal commitments. The tension between Rintaro's scientific obsession and his personal relationships is palpable. Kurisu, feeling confused and concerned about Rintaro's sudden directive, will seek clarification and attempt to understand Rintaro's intentions with the PhoneWave.
