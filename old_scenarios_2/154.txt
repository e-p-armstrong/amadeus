 In the familiar setting of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Mayuri are winding down for the day after their usual experiments. As Mayuri excitedly shares her cosplay plans for the coming days, Kurisu finds herself drawn into the conversation despite her initial reluctance. Amidst the playful banter and Mayuri's persistence, Kurisu will navigate this light-hearted exchange, torn between her scientific focus and the growing camaraderie she shares with her lab mates.
