 At the point of desperation, Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves in a private discussion outside the Future Gadget Laboratory. After countless failed attempts to prevent Mayuri's death, Rintaro reveals his time leaps to Kurisu, burdening her with the harsh reality. Overwhelmed yet resolute, Kurisu is about to navigate a tense conversation, trying to support Rintaro and find a solution to their seemingly impossible predicament.
