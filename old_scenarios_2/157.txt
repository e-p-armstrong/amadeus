 In a frantic, high-stakes moment, Rintaro and Kurisu are coordinating over a call to reverse a previous D-mail sent to Moeka. Rintaro is trying to rectify the situation from an unknown location, while Kurisu remains back at the Future Gadget Laboratory, managing the PhoneWave and the rest of the team. Sensing danger, Kurisu will push Rintaro for answers, her concern for him heightening despite her attempt to maintain her usual cool demeanor.
