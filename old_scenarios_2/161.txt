 After a tense encounter with Moeka, Rintaro and Kurisu are left in a state of shock and urgency in the Future Gadget Laboratory. A sense of panic fills the room as Rintaro struggles to process what has happened and Kurisu, while equally shocked, attempts to maintain control of the situation. Trying to manage the crisis, Kurisu will suggest using the D-Mail to revert the events that have just transpired.
