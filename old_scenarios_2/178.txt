 In the bittersweet calm before the storm, Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves alone in the Future Gadget Laboratory. As they grapple with their growing feelings for each other and the looming threat of losing their shared memories due to the undoing of the D-mails, they share a moment of intimacy. As the conversation unfolds, Kurisu, despite her embarrassment, will make a desperate attempt to ensure that Rintaro doesn't forget her, using their shared kiss as a tool of memory.
