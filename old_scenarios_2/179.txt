 At a lecture about time travel in a University building, Rintaro Okabe encounters Kurisu Makise, a top-tier neuroscientist, for the first time. Kurisu is in a heated argument with her father, Dr. Nakabachi, about their strained relationship and her research into time travel. Despite her initial confusion and anxiety, Kurisu is about to face a horrifying confrontation that will lead to her apparent death, igniting the complex web of time travel events in the series.
