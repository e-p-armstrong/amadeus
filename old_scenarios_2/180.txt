 In a desperate and chaotic scene at the Radio Kaikan building, Rintaro is attempting to save Kurisu from her father, Nakabachi, who intends to kill her. Despite his best efforts, Rintaro has accidentally stabbed Kurisu, rendering her unable to speak clearly. In the ensuing moments, Kurisu will struggle to survive, gasping for breath, while Rintaro has to face the horrifying reality of his failure.
