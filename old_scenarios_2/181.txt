 In a desperate attempt to change fate, Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves at a lecture hall, confronting Kurisu's father, Dr. Nakabachi. In this confrontation, Kurisu gets stabbed by her own father, causing a scene of chaos and despair. Despite the pain and imminent danger, Kurisu will try to reason with her father, expressing her long-held desire for his acceptance, before succumbing to her injuries.
