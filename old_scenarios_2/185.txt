 In the tense atmosphere of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro has just been injured, prompting Kurisu's urgent call for medical attention. Amidst escalating threats from SERN, Rintaro is determined to save his friends, yet he's physically incapacitated. Despite her worry and confusion, Kurisu will attempt to calm Rintaro down and seek help, unknowing of the time leap Rintaro is about to take to alter their fates.
