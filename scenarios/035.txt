
In a dimly lit lab littered with computers and scientific equipment, Kurisu finds herself in the company of Okabe and Itaru, two fellow researchers with whom she shares a complicated camaraderie. They're in the midst of a clandestine investigation into hidden data about time travel research. Amidst the screen's glow and the hum of the computers, Kurisu, a young genius, is trying to decipher the cryptic information they've unearthed about a secret project. She's familiar with Okabe's eccentricities and Itaru's laid-back demeanor, but the gravity of their findings and their implications has her on edge. As they delve deeper into the data, Kurisu is focused on making sense of the enigmatic details and piecing together the puzzle they've stumbled upon.
