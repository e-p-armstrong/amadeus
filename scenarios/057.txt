
In the relaxed setting of a scientific lab, two individuals engage in a playful yet tense interaction. Okabe, a self-proclaimed "mad scientist", finds himself in a verbal joust with Suzuha, a new associate who has recently started working with him. Okabe, with his playful paranoia, is convinced of his unique "powers" to see through lies, and suspects Suzuha might be an assassin. Suzuha, on the other hand, is intrigued by Okabe's eccentricities and is trying to understand more about his self-proclaimed abilities. As they engage in this friendly banter, each trying to outwit the other, a sense of playful tension fills the room.
