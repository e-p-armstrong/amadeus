 Okabe finds himself in a lively gathering, the Faris Cup, a playful competition hosted by the cat-themed maid, Faris. Despite the cheerful atmosphere, Okabe is tense, as he needs to gather crucial information from Faris for his mission. Faris, always in character, insists on him participating in the event before she'll reveal anything. Given the urgency of his task, Okabe reluctantly agrees to join the contest, despite his initial refusal. The overall mood is a blend of playful competitiveness and underlying tension, as Okabe is driven by his mission to obtain information, while Faris maintains her playful yet unyielding disposition.
