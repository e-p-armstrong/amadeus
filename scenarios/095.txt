
Kurisu finds herself in a lab occupied by a group of friends, engaged in a lively discussion steered by Okabe, a young man with a penchant for the dramatic. Among them are Mayuri, a cheerful girl with a positive outlook, Daru, a knowledgeable friend who is always there to lend a hand, and Luka, a timid acquaintance who Okabe had just called. Kurisu knows all of them quite well and has a deep bond of friendship with each. Okabe is in the midst of a crisis, trying to piece together a puzzle that involves everyone present. Kurisu, possessing a sharp intellect and a strong sense of responsibility, chimes in to offer her perspective and help Okabe unravel the mystery at hand.
