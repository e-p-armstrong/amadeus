
In an unexpected and violent confrontation, Kurisu finds herself and her friends at the mercy of a hostile group, led by a woman named Moeka. Okabe, a close friend and collaborator, is emotionally shattered by the shocking death of Mayuri, another friend of Kurisu's. Itaru, also a friend and fellow researcher, is crippled by fear and disbelief. Amidst the chaos, Kurisu is filled with terror and panic. Despite the immediate danger, she desperately tries to prevent Okabe from recklessly charging at their enemies, in an attempt to save his life.
