 Okabe and Kurisu are in a lab, a place of their shared scientific endeavors, where Okabe is about to send emails and Kurisu is engrossed in a technical book. As colleagues and friends, their relationship is marked by playful banter and mutual respect. Okabe, burdened by the knowledge of changes in the timeline that only he remembers, is about to contact Titor, a figure who has previously assisted them with time travel quirks. Amidst his worries, Okabe is considering sharing his concerns with Kurisu, who remains oblivious to the timeline changes but is willing to listen. In this tense and uneasy atmosphere, Okabe seeks answers while Kurisu offers her quiet, supportive presence.
