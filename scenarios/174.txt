
Kurisu is in a room filled with scientific equipment, engrossed in a hefty western book while Okabe, her fellow researcher, grapples with the potential consequences of their time travel experiments. They are joined by Mayuri, a friend of theirs who is oblivious to the gravity of the situation, and are expecting Daru, another colleague, to join them soon. Kurisu has a strong bond with these individuals as they have been through countless adventures together. As Okabe contemplates using the Time Leap Machine to prevent a potential tragedy, Kurisu, noticing his troubled demeanor, is about to question him about his intentions.
