
Kurisu finds herself in an unusual setting; a maid café bustling with novelty and intrigue. She is accompanied by Okabe, whom she regularly interacts with, and Faris, a bubbly and playful maiden of the café whom she's meeting for the first time. As she observes the unique customs of the café, a playful conversation about Okabe's romantic prospects unfolds, piquing her interest. Intrigued by the playful banter and wanting to better understand the dynamics among Okabe and his friends, she decides to join the conversation.
