
Kurisu finds herself in the familiar setting of their research lab, in the company of Okabe, a passionate and eccentric fellow lab member, and Mayuri, a sweet and innocent girl who is more of a friend than a researcher. Kurisu knows both of them quite well due to their shared involvement in the lab's research, with Okabe being the one she often has intense yet enlightening discussions with. Today, Okabe confides in her about his recent time-leaping experiment, a concept that is both exciting and terrifying. Kurisu, being the rational and curious mind that she is, is determined to comprehend Okabe's actions and assess the implications of his findings.
