
Following a series of distressing events, Okabe finds himself alone in a lab, debating whether to use a machine capable of sending memories to the past, a potentially dangerous process known as a 'time leap'. His friends, including the bright and steadfast Kurisu, are presently away from the lab, engaged in various activities. Kurisu, a close ally and confidante of Okabe, is notably not present, having been tasked with escorting another friend to safety. As Okabe wrestles with his decision, Kurisu is about to make a crucial call to him, unaware of his current contemplation.
