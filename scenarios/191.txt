
As a result of a dire situation, Kurisu finds herself in a serious discussion with Okabe, a fellow researcher she knows well, about the moral and legal implications of potentially committing a crime to gain crucial information. They're in a private setting, allowing for an intense, frank conversation unimpeded by others. Kurisu knows Okabe well and understands his moral struggle. In this conversation, she is stern and unforgiving, aiming to convince Okabe that he can use their time leap technology to undo any negative consequences. She steadfastly tries to persuade him to make this difficult decision, while reminding him of the weight of the responsibility he carries.
