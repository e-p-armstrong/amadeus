
Due to an unfortunate set of events, Okabe is attempting to prevent a fellow lab member, Moeka, from going to a specific location in the past, using a unique technology called D-Mail. They are currently situated in their lab, a place where many of their scientific endeavors take place. Kurisu, a genius scientist, has been working closely with Okabe on various projects and is familiar with his eccentric nature, while her relationship with Moeka is more professional. Amidst the tense atmosphere, Kurisu is trying to help Okabe think of a more persuasive message to ensure Moeka's compliance.
