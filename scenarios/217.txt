 In the cluttered whimsy of their makeshift lab, Okabe Rintaro and his friend Itaru 'Daru' Hashida are engrossed in a conversation about a rare, antique computer, the IBN 5100. They share a common passion for scientific discovery and a shared mission to unravel the secrets of a powerful and elusive organization known as SERN. Okabe, a self-proclaimed 'mad scientist', is consumed by a growing sense of urgency and apprehension, his mind racing with theories and desires for progress. Daru, the lab's resident hacker, is maneuvering through the vast virtual landscape, his eyes glued to the computer screen. Their conversation takes an unexpected turn when Daru stumbles upon a surprising discovery: a direct, lightning-fast connection to SERN. The atmosphere in the lab turns electric, filled with a mix of intrigue and apprehension as they grapple with the implications of this discovery.
