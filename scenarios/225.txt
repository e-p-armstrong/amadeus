 Okabe and Kurisu, two accomplished scientists, find themselves in the quiet of their shared lab following a distressing phone call Kurisu had with her estranged father. Their relationship, usually characterized by intellectual banter and mutual respect, takes a turn into more personal territory as Kurisu grapples with her troubled past. Okabe, having glimpsed Kurisu's vulnerability, delicately navigates a conversation about her father. He attempts to understand and comfort her, while Kurisu struggles to reconcile her feelings about her father, her past, and her own genius. The air is heavy with unspoken emotions as they confront the complexities of family relationships and personal identity.
