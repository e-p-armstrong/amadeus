 In the bustling city of Akihabara, a group of friends stand by a time machine, preparing to bid farewell to their friend Suzuha, who is about to embark on a journey to the past. Suzuha, a time traveler, is the daughter of Daru, who is unaware of her identity. She is ready to undertake her mission, while Okabe, Daru, Mayuri, and Kurisu are filled with a mix of anxiety and resolve, having been entrusted with the responsibility of changing the future. As Suzuha steps into the time machine, she leaves them with a task: to alter the timeline and prevent a dystopian future. Each of them silently vows to see this mission through, their faces reflecting a solemn determination. The air is filled with a bittersweet mood as they prepare to say goodbye, their resolve to change the future never wavering.
