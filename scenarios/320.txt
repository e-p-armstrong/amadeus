 In a lab filled with tension, Okabe, Kurisu, and Mayuri grapple with a crucial decision. As scientists and close friends, Okabe and Kurisu have pushed the boundaries of time itself, creating a device, dubbed the "PhoneWave," capable of sending messages, or "D-Mails," back in time. Now, Okabe stands on the precipice of using this technology to change past events, driven by a desperate need to avert a future disaster. However, the fear of erasing cherished memories and relationships looms heavy in the air, evidenced by Kurisu's fearful inquiries and Mayuri's tearful objections. As Okabe wrestles with his decision, the lab echoes with the melancholy of potential loss and the relentless ticking of time.
