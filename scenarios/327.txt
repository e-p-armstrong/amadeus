
Inside a cluttered, science-laden laboratory, Okabe, a self-proclaimed mad scientist, is in a rush against time with his close friends Mayuri, Kurisu, and Daru. They've built strong bonds through shared scientific endeavors, and now they face a terrifying test of their camaraderie. Okabe is filled with desperation and fear as he attempts to operate a device they've invented, the Time Leap Machine, which has the ability to send a person's memories back in time. As the lab is besieged by an aggressive group known as the Rounders, Mayuri is hit by a stray bullet and Okabe is forced to leap back in time to avert this tragedy. The overall atmosphere is one of frantic desperation as Okabe tries to rescue his friends, while Kurisu and Daru are left behind to fend off their attackers.
