 Outside the UPX assembly hall, Okabe anxiously awaits the arrival of his friend Faris, who is about to participate in a crucial game tournament. Their relationship, usually characterized by playful banter, takes a serious turn when Okabe reveals he's brought sunglasses and earplugs for Faris. Okabe, having traveled from the future with the knowledge of Faris's impending loss due to external interference, is determined to alter this outcome. Faris, initially taken aback and slightly nervous about the game, is thrown into confusion by Okabe's grave demeanor and unexpected revelation. As the crowd swells around them, the tension mounts: Okabe hopes to change the future, while Faris confronts the weight of his words and the upcoming match. The scene is charged with a mix of apprehension and determination.
