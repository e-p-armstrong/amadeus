 Okabe and Faris are hiding in a dimly lit storehouse, the tension between them palpable. Having discovered that they could alter the past with messages referred to as "D-Mails", Okabe is desperate to undo the changes, as they have led to unfavorable outcomes. Faris, however, is distraught at the prospect of cancelling her D-Mail, which saved her father's life a decade ago. In this tense situation, Okabe is struggling to convince Faris to resend her D-Mail and 'kill' her father again to restore the original timeline, while Faris is adamant in her refusal. The scene is filled with desperation, fear, and the painful struggle between personal loss and the greater good.
