
In the privacy of Luka's home, an uncomfortable conversation unfolds between the young, shy Luka, her father, and the eccentric Okabe Rintaro. Okabe, who once acted as a mentor to Luka, has recently created tension by crossing personal boundaries, leaving both of them feeling uneasy. Despite his self-consciousness and regret, Okabe is also driven by his search for an old computer model, the IBN 5100, which he believes holds crucial information. The interaction is strained, as Okabe attempts to navigate the complicated dynamics with Luka, while also pressing her and her father about the computer. His goal is to acquire the IBN 5100 without further damaging his relationship with Luka. The scene is characterized by discomfort and desperation, as Okabe grapples with his mistakes and the elusive information he seeks.
