 Okabe Rintaro and Moeka are in a nondescript location, their relationship strained by recent revelations of Moeka's deception. Moeka had previously manipulated Okabe into revealing the location of a rare vintage computer, the IBN 5100, causing the timeline to shift. Now, Okabe, feeling betrayed yet determined, plans to undo the manipulation by cancelling a message Moeka had sent in the past. Meanwhile, Moeka is visibly upset and remorseful. Concurrently, Okabe is in contact with Kurisu, a close friend who is worried about his wellbeing. The mood is tense, filled with regret and a strong sense of determination.
