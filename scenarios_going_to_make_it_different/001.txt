 At a lecture hall on time travel, Rintaro is shocked to see Kurisu alive and well, as he previously discovered her lifeless body right before experiencing a strange event that seemed to rewrite reality. Kurisu is unfamiliar with both Rintaro and Itaru at this point, being taken aback by Rintaro's outbursts and accusations. As the lecture progresses, Kurisu will attempt to defend her stance on time travel against Rintaro's fervent objections, unknowingly setting the stage for her eventual collaboration with him.
