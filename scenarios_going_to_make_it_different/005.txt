 Having stumbled upon the Future Gadget Laboratory and the eccentric team within, Kurisu is curious about the bizarre phenomenon she has witnessed with the PhoneWave. She is familiar with Rintaro and Itaru, but they are still relatively new acquaintances and she's wary of their odd behavior. Determined to understand the strange device, Kurisu will reluctantly agree to Rintaro's terms, joining the lab and setting the stage for her deeper involvement in the unfolding time travel experiments.
