 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu confronts Rintaro and Itaru about their unusual behavior, and in particular, the strange occurrence involving the PhoneWave and a banana. After enduring Rintaro's teasing and eccentricity, she expresses a genuine desire to understand what's happening. As this conversation proceeds, Kurisu will reluctantly agree to Rintaro's conditions to join the lab, marking the beginning of her active involvement in the time travel experiments.
