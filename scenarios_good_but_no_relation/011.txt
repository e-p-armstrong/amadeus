 A typical day at the Future Gadget Lab finds Kurisu and Rintaro in a mix of scientific discussion and playful banter, indicating their growing camaraderie. The satellite crash into Radi-Kan is a recent memory, and the mystery of the PhoneWave's capabilities is still being unravelled. In this conversation, Kurisu, despite her internal doubts and external irritation with Rintaro's eccentricities, will dive back into their ongoing investigation of the PhoneWave's strange phenomena.
