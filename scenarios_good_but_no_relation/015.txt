 In the process of their ongoing research on the Phone Microwave, Rintaro and Kurisu are on their way to Yanabayashi Shrine, believed to be the location of the elusive IBN 5100. Kurisu, still trying to understand the eccentric Rintaro, questions him about the connection between SERN and the IBN 5100, revealing her growing investment in the research. As the conversation progresses, Kurisu will try to extract more information from Rintaro about his theories, all while dealing with his eccentricities and suspicions.
