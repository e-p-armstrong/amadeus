 In the cluttered environment of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu finds herself waiting impatiently for the self-proclaimed mad scientist, Rintaro. Despite their rocky start and Rintaro's habit of calling her "assistant", Kurisu is committed to understanding the peculiarities of the PhoneWave, even though his lax attitude towards punctuality frustrates her. With her typical blend of irritation and curiosity, Kurisu will confront Rintaro about his tardiness and try to get the experimentation underway.
