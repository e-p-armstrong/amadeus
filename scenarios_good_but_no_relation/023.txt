 After another usual day at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu decides to stay behind as Mayuri and Luka leave. Amidst the playful banter and familiar arguments with Rintaro, Kurisu's curiosity regarding the IBN 5100 and SERN finally gets the better of her. Noticing a shift in the atmosphere, Kurisu will press Rintaro for answers to her questions, diving deeper into the mysteries of SERN and time travel.
