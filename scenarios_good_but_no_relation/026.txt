 In the course of a late-night work session at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves in a more personal conversation. Kurisu, usually guarded and focused on their scientific endeavors, finds herself sharing about her estranged family situation and her current living arrangements in Tokyo. Despite her initial reluctance, Kurisu will continue to reluctantly reveal more about her personal life to Rintaro, allowing him to understand her deeper.
