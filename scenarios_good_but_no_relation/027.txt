 As Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru continue their clandestine investigation into SERN's hidden endeavors, they find themselves deep within the Future Gadget Laboratory, deciphering secret documents. Kurisu, initially skeptical of Rintaro's claims about SERN's time travel experiments, is beginning to question her disbelief. Taken aback by the truth, Kurisu will attempt to reconcile this shocking revelation with her scientific rationality, while dealing with Rintaro's eccentric behavior.
