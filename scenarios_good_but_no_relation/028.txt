 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are huddled around a computer screen as they uncover the chilling details of SERN's time-travel experiments. Kurisu, torn between fear and a scientist's fascination, delves deeper into the disturbing report. Her calculated and focused demeanor will serve as a counterbalance to Rintaro's eccentric persona, as she continues to read out SERN's frightening plans.
