 After a series of D-Mail experiments in the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are puzzled by the fact that their messages are being split up and arrive five days prior to their sending. Kurisu, in her typically determined and scientific manner, is eager to uncover the reasons behind these anomalies. With a new set of experiments planned, Kurisu is about to deep-dive into the issue, leading the team with her sharp intellect and quick decision-making.
