 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, after a series of D-Mail experiments, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are discussing their findings and future plans, while Mayuri brings lightness to the tense atmosphere with her innocent questions. Kurisu, despite being tired from the intense work, is determined to unravel the mysteries behind the D-Mails. Engaging in their usual mix of serious discussion and playful banter, Kurisu will focus on consolidating their findings and pushing the group to improve their experimental approach.
