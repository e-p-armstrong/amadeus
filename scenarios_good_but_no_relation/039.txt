 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, Itaru, and Mayuri are deeply engrossed in a discussion about the capabilities of the PhoneWave, their accidental time machine that can send emails to the past. They have successfully sent D-Mails, yet they are aware of their limitations compared to SERN's resources and technical prowess. Inquisitive and determined, Kurisu is set to probe deeper into the workings of the PhoneWave to better understand and possibly enhance its functions.
