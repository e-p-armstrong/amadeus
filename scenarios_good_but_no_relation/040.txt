 In the cluttered space of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, Itaru, and Mayuri are in a discussion about the unexpected time travel capabilities of the PhoneWave. Feeling overwhelmed and slightly frustrated by the uncertainty, Kurisu is trying to make sense of the accidental discovery. Contemplating the reality of time travel, Kurisu will soon head back to her hotel to rest and process the day's revelations.
