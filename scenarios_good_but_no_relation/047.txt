 In the bustling Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are in the midst of a lively discussion. Having joined the lab and interacted with Rintaro for a while, Kurisu is not as formal as she was at the start, but is still irked by Rintaro's playful nickname for her. Feeling irked, Kurisu is about to challenge Rintaro's teasing in her usual sarcastic manner.
