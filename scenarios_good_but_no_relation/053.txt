 The Future Gadget Laboratory is alive with friendly banter, as Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru continue their work on perfecting the PhoneWave. The atmosphere is light and full of camaraderie, a departure from their initial tense interactions, indicating that Kurisu has grown closer to the lab members. Frustrated yet amused, Kurisu is about to challenge Itaru's self-proclaimed title of a 'pervy gentleman', trying to understand what he means by that.
