 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru, along with Mayuri, are dealing with the unexpected effects of a D-Mail sent by someone else (Kiryu Moeka). Kurisu, feeling a mix of curiosity and apprehension, is trying to make sense of the situation using her scientific knowledge. Focusing on the issue at hand, Kurisu will attempt to explain the concept of a time paradox to Rintaro and the others, all while trying to understand the unexpected effects of the D-Mail.
