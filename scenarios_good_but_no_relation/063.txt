 After a series of D-mail experiments, the Future Gadget Lab members, including Kurisu, are trying to understand the ramifications of the changes they've made to the past. Kurisu is particularly puzzled by Rintaro's unique ability to remember events across different timelines, which he dubs "Reading Steiner". As the conversation continues, Kurisu will challenge Rintaro's claims and assumptions, attempting to unravel the mystery behind the phenomena they are dealing with.
