 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and the other lab members are discussing the possibilities of their newfound ability to send D-mails. Kurisu, initially skeptical and surprised by Luka's wish to change their gender, will soon find herself grappling with her own insecurities as she compares herself to Luka, and will engage in a heated, humorous argument with Rintaro.
