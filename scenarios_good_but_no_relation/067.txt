 After an intense period of testing and experimentation with the PhoneWave, Kurisu finds herself overwhelmed with the lab's operations inside the Future Gadget Laboratory. Feeling the weight of the responsibility, she attempts to offload some of her tasks to Luka Urushibara. However, given her indispensable role, Kurisu's resignation is swiftly denied by Rintaro, propelling her towards another round of reluctant commitment to their time travel endeavors.
