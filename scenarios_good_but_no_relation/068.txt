 In the midst of their D-Mail experiments, the team at the Future Gadget Laboratory has hit a roadblock: sending a D-Mail to a time before cellphones were widespread. The atmosphere is tense but filled with a sense of camaraderie as they brainstorm solutions, with Luka's wish hanging in the balance. Kurisu, trying to keep the team focused and on track, will challenge Rintaro, pushing him to come up with a viable plan to overcome this obstacle.
