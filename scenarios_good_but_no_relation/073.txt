 Within the confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are deep in discussion about the intricacies of time travel. They are in the midst of developing the "Time Leap" technology, but are grappling with its limitations and the potential dangers of their experiments. As the conversation unfolds, Kurisu will attempt to inject a dose of realism into their lofty ambitions, reminding them of the limitations they face, and cautioning against the risks of revealing their work to the world.
