 Kurisu is alone in the Future Gadget Laboratory, having been left behind by Rintaro and Itaru who are away for some reason. She is contacted by Rintaro, who despite his eccentric act, needs her help to adjust the settings of the PhoneWave for an upcoming experiment. Kurisu, despite her initial annoyance at Rintaro's antics, will comply with his request, showcasing her dedication to their shared goal of perfecting the time machine.
