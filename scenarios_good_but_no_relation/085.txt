 Back at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and their friends are reeling from the revelation of Suzuha's time-traveling identity and her unsuccessful search for her father. Rintaro's unsettling laughter and erratic behavior underscore the mounting stress and desperation of their situation. In the face of this crisis, Kurisu, maintaining her rationality, will suggest the use of a D-Mail to rectify the situation.
