 In the midst of a brainstorming session in the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are grappling with the weighty implications of their newly discovered ability to send memories to the past. As they deliberate over the potential dangers and ethical dilemmas of their invention, Kurisu is feeling a mix of excitement and apprehension about the possibilities. As the conversation progresses, Kurisu will engage Rintaro about her personal concerns, revealing more about her strained relationship with her father.
