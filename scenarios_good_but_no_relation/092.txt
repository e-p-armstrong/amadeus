 In the laid-back environment of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu and Rintaro are enjoying a moment of levity amidst their intense research on the PhoneWave. Kurisu, despite her usual composed demeanor, is flustered after being caught using @channel memes, a popular Internet forum in Japan. In the ensuing conversation, she will try to keep her cool and convince Rintaro to keep her online activities a secret.
