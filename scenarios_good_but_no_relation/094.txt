 After an afternoon of research and testing in the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Mayuri, and Rintaro find themselves in a playful, yet tense situation. Despite the jovial atmosphere, they are on the brink of another experimental push with the PhoneWave, and the pressure is building. Amidst her frustration and the hilarity of the situation, Kurisu will urge Rintaro to take the lead, trying to keep the work on track and progress with the PhoneWave's development.
