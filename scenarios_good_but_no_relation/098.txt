 Kurisu, Rintaro, Itaru, and Mayuri are at the Future Gadget Laboratory, discussing the potential consequences of using the newly-developed Time Leap Machine. Kurisu, skeptical and apprehensive about the unknown, suggests turning the machine over to professionals, but Rintaro is keen on experimenting. Despite her trepidation, Kurisu will engage in a lengthy, detailed discussion about the mechanics and theoretical implications of time leaping, trying to alleviate the team's fears.
