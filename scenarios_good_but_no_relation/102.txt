 The Future Gadget Laboratory is under siege by SERN, with Rintaro, Kurisu, and their friends caught in the crossfire. In a desperate attempt to change the dire situation, Rintaro declares his intent to time-leap and save Mayuri, despite the risks and Kurisu's objections. Kurisu, deeply worried and conflicted, will challenge Rintaro's decision, questioning him fervently as she fears for his safety, and the future they might be creating.
