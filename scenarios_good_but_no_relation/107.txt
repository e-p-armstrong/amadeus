 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are under attack from SERN, with Rintaro desperately trying to use the Time Leap Machine to save Mayuri. Kurisu, not yet aware of Rintaro's ability to retain his memories across timelines, is confused and concerned about his erratic behaviour and sudden urgency. In the ensuing chaos, Kurisu will try to understand Rintaro's actions while grappling with her own fear and confusion.
