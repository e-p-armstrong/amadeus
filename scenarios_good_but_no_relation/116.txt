 Within the confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are grappling with the harsh realities revealed by Suzuha about the dystopian future ruled by SERN. Kurisu is in disbelief and shock upon learning of her future collaboration with SERN and her early death, considering her strong disdain for their unethical practices. As she digests this news, Kurisu will anxiously question Suzuha further, trying to understand the circumstances that led to her future actions and death.
