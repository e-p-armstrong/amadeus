 In the aftermath of Suzuha's reveal and the subsequent malfunction of her time machine, Kurisu, Rintaro, and the rest of the Future Gadget Laboratory members find themselves in a predicament. Kurisu, displaying her problem-solving skills, is trying to devise a way around their current situation. She will propose using the Time Leap Machine to revert to a timeline prior to the breaking of Suzuha's time machine, displaying her quick thinking and initiative.
