 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Mayuri are listening to Suzuha as she shares details about her mysterious past, having just revealed her identity as a time traveler. Kurisu, ever the scientist, is trying to make sense of the information and find leads to help Suzuha. In the coming conversation, Kurisu will try to piece together clues from Suzuha's story, seeking a rational explanation for the seemingly impossible time travel.
