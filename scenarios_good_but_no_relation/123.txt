 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, after yet another time leap, a weary Rintaro awakens to find Kurisu by his side, the remnants of his failed attempts at saving Mayuri lingering in the air. Kurisu, showing her caring side underneath her usual blunt demeanor, has been watching over Rintaro, concerned for his well-being amidst his unending struggle. Sensing Rintaro's despair, she will attempt to offer him comfort and support, even as they continue their desperate attempts to alter fate.
