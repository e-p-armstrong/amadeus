 In the midst of the usual day-to-day activities at the Future Gadget Laboratory, the group is struck by a bombshell revelation when Mayuri innocently points out the resemblance between Suzuha and Itaru. As the truth clicks into place, the team is shocked and bewildered, grappling with the reality of Suzuha being Itaru's daughter. In the ensuing conversation, Kurisu will try to bring some semblance of normalcy back to the situation with her usual sarcastic commentary.
