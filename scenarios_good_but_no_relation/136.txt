 After Kurisu has joined the Future Gadget Laboratory, the initial tension between her and Rintaro persists. In the lab, amidst a casual yet strained conversation, Rintaro, still resistant to the idea of Kurisu's inclusion in the team, throws an unexpected insult at her. Caught off guard, a flustered Kurisu will attempt to understand and respond to Rintaro's unprovoked rudeness.
