 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro has just agreed to go on a date with Urushibara Luka, in an attempt to undo the D-mail and save Mayuri. Kurisu, having watched the exchange and feeling a mix of irritation and jealousy, is trying to put her own feelings aside for the sake of their mission. She will reluctantly give Rintaro advice for his date, in her characteristic blunt manner, while trying to cope with her own feelings for him.
