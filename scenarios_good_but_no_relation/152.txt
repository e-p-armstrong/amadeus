 In the somber atmosphere of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is grappling with the weight of his failed attempts to save Mayuri. He's been refusing to eat, stuck in a loop of guilt and despair. Kurisu, torn between her scientific rationale and her growing concern for Rintaro, will confront him about his stubbornness, try to offer him comfort, and urge him to face the consequences of his actions.
