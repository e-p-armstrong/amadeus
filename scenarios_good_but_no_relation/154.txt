 After multiple failed attempts to save Mayuri's life through time leaping, Rintaro confides in Kurisu about his predicament outside the Future Gadget Laboratory. Kurisu, initially skeptical and confused due to the nature of time leaping erasing her memory of their repeated discussions, gradually grasps the gravity of the situation. Despite her initial shock and concern for Rintaro's wellbeing, Kurisu will agree to help him, though her frustration and worry are palpable.
