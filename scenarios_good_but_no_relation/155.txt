 At this point in the story, Rintaro is at Moeka's apartment, on a mission to undo the D-mail that she had sent earlier, this being the only way to save Mayuri's life. Back at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu anxiously awaits updates, her usual scientific curiosity replaced by a sense of urgency and concern for her friends. As the conversation progresses, Kurisu will navigate her own conflicting emotions, providing Rintaro with the brutal but necessary advice he needs to accomplish his mission.
