 In an attempt to restore the original timeline and save Mayuri, Rintaro and Kurisu are frantically working to undo the D-mails they had sent earlier. This conversation takes place in the midst of their tense operation, with Rintaro in danger while trying to reverse the D-mail sent to Moeka. Kurisu, worried about Rintaro's safety yet determined to help him, will continue assisting him to undo the D-mails, amidst the chaos and possible threats from SERN.
