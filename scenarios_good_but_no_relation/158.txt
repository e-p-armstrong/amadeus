 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu confronts Rintaro about his secretive behavior, concerned about the effect his actions are having on Mayuri. Rintaro, in his desperate attempts to reverse the D-mails, has been keeping Kurisu in the dark, causing her to worry about both him and Mayuri. Despite her concerns and the dangerous implications, Kurisu will insist on accompanying Rintaro, determined to stand by his side to face whatever challenges they may encounter.
