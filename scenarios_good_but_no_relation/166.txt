 Following the tragic death of Mayuri and Rintaro's unsuccessful attempts to save her through time leaps, tension is high in the Future Gadget Laboratory. Rintaro, fuelled by desperation, suggests hacking into SERN, alarming Kurisu who is concerned about the consequences of such a reckless act. Kurisu, both worried and apprehensive, will try to dissuade Rintaro from his risky plan, fearing the potential danger they could all face.
