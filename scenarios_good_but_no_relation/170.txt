 After failed attempts at time-leaping to save Mayuri's life from the relentless pursuit of SERN, Rintaro is racked with guilt and despair in the Future Gadget Lab. Kurisu, concerned and wishing to help Rintaro, tries to provide him with emotional support and courage. Intent on alleviating Rintaro's guilt and fear, Kurisu will firmly urge him to confront Mayuri and express his feelings, embodying her role as the lab's source of emotional strength and guidance.
