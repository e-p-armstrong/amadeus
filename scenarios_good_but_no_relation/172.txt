 In a quiet, secluded corner of Akihabara, Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves in a deep conversation about the repeated time leaps made to save Mayuri. Kurisu, having witnessed Rintaro's relentless efforts and suffering, feels a mix of worry, fear, and admiration for him. The conversation will take a turn towards Kurisu expressing her own fears about being left behind in the timeline shifts, showing her vulnerability to Rintaro for the first time.
