 In a quiet corner of Akihabara, Rintaro and Kurisu have a heartfelt conversation about the difficult decision that lies ahead. Kurisu, despite her own feelings, is resolutely urging Rintaro to choose a timeline where Mayuri survives, sacrificing herself in the process. Tearful but determined, Kurisu is set on heading back to the lab, aiming to put on a brave face and maintain a sense of normalcy amidst the looming crisis.
