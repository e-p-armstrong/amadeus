 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu is getting ready to say her final goodbye to Rintaro and Mayuri, her newfound friends, before Rintaro undoes the D-mail that resulted in her survival. The atmosphere is thick with emotion, as Kurisu tries to lighten the mood by pretending she's merely going back to America. Kurisu, feeling a mix of sadness and determination, will make her farewell as light-hearted as possible, masking her true feelings to lessen the pain of her departure.
