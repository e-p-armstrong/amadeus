 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu and Rintaro are in a heated discussion about the consequences of their time travel experiments. Kurisu is feeling a mixture of frustration, sadness, and resignation as she tries to persuade Rintaro to accept the unavoidable: the choice between her life and Mayuri's. Despite her strong exterior, Kurisu is also quietly desperate to know if she will be remembered in the Beta worldline, and will soon try to hide her vulnerability behind scientific jargon.
