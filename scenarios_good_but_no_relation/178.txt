 In a lecture hall, Kurisu is attending a conference on time travel, a reunion with her estranged father, Dr. Nakabachi, whom she hasn't spoken to in seven years. She's feeling anxious but hopeful about reconnecting with him and is trying to open up a dialogue by sharing her thesis on time travel. Attempting to mend the strained relationship, Kurisu will attempt to engage her father in a scientific discussion, hoping to find common ground.
