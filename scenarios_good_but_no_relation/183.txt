 In a tense confrontation at the Radio Kaikan building, Rintaro faces off against Doctor Nakabachi, Kurisu's bitter father, in a desperate bid to save Kurisu without altering the timeline. Kurisu is terrified, caught in the middle of a deadly struggle between her father and Rintaro, the man she has grown to care for deeply. As the situation escalates, Kurisu will desperately intervene to prevent any bloodshed, placing herself in harm's way to protect Rintaro from her father's violent rage.
