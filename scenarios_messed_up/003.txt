 At a lecture on the subject of time travel in a university hall, the genius scientist Kurisu Makise is explaining the complexities and paradoxes of hypothetical time travel to an audience that includes Rintaro Okabe. Kurisu, feeling challenged yet excited to share her knowledge, is engaging the audience in a detailed explanation of her theories. As the lecture progresses, Kurisu will further delve into the principles of causality and the Grandfather Paradox, setting the stage for a contentious interaction with Rintaro.
