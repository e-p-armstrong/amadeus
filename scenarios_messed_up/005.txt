 In the cluttered confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu confronts Rintaro and Itaru about their odd behavior and the peculiar nature of the PhoneWave. Despite her apprehension and frustration with their antics, she's intrigued by the device and the strange phenomenon it's caused, enough to consider joining their team. Kurisu, feeling both exasperated and curious, will proceed to negotiate the terms of her potential membership in the lab.
