 After Rintaro's unplanned activation of the PhoneWave, he, Kurisu, and Itaru are back in the Future Gadget Laboratory, attempting to make sense of the bizarre occurrences. Kurisu, skeptical and slightly irritated by Rintaro's theatrics, is trying to keep the conversation grounded in empirical data and logical deduction. Despite her exasperation, Kurisu will persist in asking probing questions to help unravel the mystery behind the PhoneWave's unexpected behavior.
