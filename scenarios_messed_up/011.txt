 In the early stages of their acquaintance, Kurisu stops by the Future Gadget Laboratory, where she finds Rintaro immersed in his intricate world of conspiracy theories and scientific exploration. The tension and unfamiliarity between them is palpable as Kurisu tries to understand Rintaro's eccentric behavior and the workings of the lab. Curious yet skeptical, Kurisu is about to question Rintaro about their unexpected results with the PhoneWave, attempting to rationalize their findings.
