 Kurisu, Rintaro, and Luka are at the Yanabayashi Shrine, working on transporting a heavy piece of equipment related to their PhoneWave experiments. This is a scene of camaraderie and light-hearted banter as they navigate the challenges of their experiment. As the conversation unfolds, Kurisu will grapple with the physical demands of science outside of her usual theoretical work, while trying to maintain her dignity and assert her boundaries.
