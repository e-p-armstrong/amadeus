 After a successful search for the rare IBN 5100 computer, Kurisu and Rintaro are exerting themselves to transport it back to the Future Gadget Laboratory, their shared burden leading to a comedic exchange of banter. Kurisu is both annoyed and slightly amused by Rintaro's antics, yet she continues to assist him despite her complaints. The arrival of Suzuha and her unexpected hostility towards Kurisu will prompt Kurisu to question the nature of their relationship, while also trying to understand Rintaro's growing obsession with fighting SERN.
