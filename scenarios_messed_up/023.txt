 Within the confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are deep in the throes of their time travel research, having already made startling discoveries using D-mails. Kurisu, despite her initial skepticism, has grown increasingly intrigued by the experiments and their implications, especially regarding SERN and the mysterious IBN 5100. As the evening grows late, Kurisu will press Rintaro and Itaru for more details about their findings, setting off a heated and enlightening discussion.
