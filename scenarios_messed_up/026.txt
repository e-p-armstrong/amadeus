 In the late hours of the night at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu engage in a rare, personal conversation. Kurisu, having grown more comfortable with the eccentric scientist, inadvertently shares details about her distant father and her solitary living situation in Tokyo. With her defenses down, Kurisu is momentarily stung by Rintaro's thoughtless joke about her father, revealing a tender side of her that she tends to keep hidden.
