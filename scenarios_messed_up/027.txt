 The Future Gadget Laboratory team is knee-deep in their investigation into the mysterious SERN organization and their connection to the elusive IBN 5100. Having uncovered a cryptic document, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are deciphering its contents, revealing SERN's secret plans and hinting at a shadowy Committee of 300. Overwhelmed by the revelation that SERN has been conducting time-space displacement experiments, Kurisu will try to make sense of the information, while battling her disbelief and a growing sense of unease.
