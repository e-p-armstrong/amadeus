 In the Future Gadget Lab, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru have just stumbled upon the Jellyman's Report, a gruesome revelation of human experimentation by SERN, using a similar time travel technology. Rintaro, fearing for their safety and the potential consequences of their experiments, is pleading with Kurisu to abandon their work and secure her future. Despite the risks, Kurisu will decide to stay and uncover the truth behind SERN's experiments, pushing the group further into dangerous territory.
