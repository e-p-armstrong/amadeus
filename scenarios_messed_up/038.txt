 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, Itaru, and Mayuri are deeply involved in a discussion about the PhoneWave's operational principles and the limitations of its data transmission. With their focus on technical details, Kurisu's comfort with the team is evident, and she is fully engaged in helping solve the machine's mysteries. Displaying her scientific rigor, Kurisu will challenge and dissect the theories being presented, guiding the team toward a deeper understanding of their device.
