 Amidst the clutter and chaos of the Future Gadget Lab, Rintaro has just sent another D-Mail, altering the timeline once again. Having no memory of this event due to the shift, Kurisu is confused and disoriented, doubting her own observations. As Rintaro tries to explain, Kurisu will struggle to understand the implications of the D-Mail and the changes it has brought, while grappling with the unsettling feeling of missing memories.
