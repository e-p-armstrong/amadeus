 After a series of successful experiments, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are back in the Future Gadget Laboratory running tests on the PhoneWave's ability to send D-mails. As the team prepares for their next experiment, dubbed Operation Urd, Kurisu expresses her growing unease about the ethical implications of altering the past. Despite her reservations, she will soon find herself caught up in the excitement and scientific potential of their experiments, even though she remains wary about the consequences.
