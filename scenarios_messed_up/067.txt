 In the bustling environment of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu is feeling the weight of the tasks she's been handling, trying to perfect the Phone Microwave and manage the unpredictable group of friends. As she seeks to lighten her load by passing her duties onto Urushibara Luka, she's met with a firm denial from Rintaro. Despite her frustration, Kurisu will continue to work diligently on their time-travel innovation, driven by her scientific curiosity and growing attachment to the team.
