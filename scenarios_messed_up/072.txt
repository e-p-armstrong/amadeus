 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, amidst the sweltering summer heat, Kurisu, Rintaro, Itaru, and Mayuri are gathered to discuss the implications and limitations of their D-Mail technology. Despite her complaints about the heat and the company, Kurisu is deeply engaged in the scientific discourse, showcasing her dedication to their shared goal. As the conversation shifts towards the possibility of physical time travel, Kurisu will voice her skepticism, highlighting the enormity of the challenge they are considering.
