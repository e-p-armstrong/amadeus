 In the intimate confines of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu share a rare moment of vulnerability after numerous shared experiments and time leaps. Kurisu, usually reserved and sarcastic, finds herself opening up to Rintaro about her feelings of friendship and the importance of their bond. As the conversation progresses, Kurisu will attempt to express her deeper feelings for Rintaro, only to be thrown off by his typical clueless response, resorting to her familiar defensiveness.
