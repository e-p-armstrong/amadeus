 In the cluttered Future Gadget Lab, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are deep into a scientific debate about the feasibility and mechanics of the PhoneWave, their time-travel machine prototype. With the looming threat of SERN, they are discussing the challenges to beat the organization to perfecting time travel. Kurisu, being a realist and concerned about the risks, will voice her doubts and caution the team about making assumptions without verification.
