 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru are discussing the intriguing potential of the PhoneWave. Kurisu is trying to bridge the gap between neuroscience and physics, explaining how her research can be used to convert memories into data that could potentially be sent back in time. With excitement and a hint of apprehension, she will proceed to lay out the groundwork for what they will later name the 'Time Leap'.
