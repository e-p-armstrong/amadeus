 In the cluttered space of the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Mayuri are caught in a goofy mishap, with Rintaro causing a ruckus. As the dust settles, they transition to a more critical task, working on the final adjustments of the Time Leap Machine. Facing a challenge that requires Rintaro's intervention, Kurisu is feeling apprehensive and will, albeit reluctantly, send Rintaro to handle the situation due to her strained relations with Moeka.
