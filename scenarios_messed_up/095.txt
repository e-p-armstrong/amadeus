 In the Future Gadget Lab, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Itaru have just made a significant discovery in their time-leap experiments involving the PhoneWave and the 42-inch CRT. Kurisu, though initially skeptical, is impressed by Rintaro's insight and attempts to motivate him to continue with their experiment. Ready to move forward, she prepares herself to convince Rintaro to take the next crucial step in their time-leap experiment.
