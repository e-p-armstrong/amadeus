 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Mayuri, Itaru, and Kurisu are celebrating the completion of their newest invention - a device that can convert memories to data and send them to the past. The atmosphere is one of accomplishment and camaraderie, with a friendly debate over what to name the device. Kurisu, feeling a mix of satisfaction and apprehension, will explain the working of the Time Leap Machine in detail, marking the start of a new chapter in their time travel experiments.
