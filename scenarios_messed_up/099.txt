 The Future Gadget Lab team, having successfully developed the Time Leap Machine, is faced with the weighty decision of whether to experiment themselves or to hand over their groundbreaking invention to a research institution. Kurisu, despite her scientific curiosity, is ultimately relieved at the choice to not personally experiment with the machine, revealing her underlying ethical concerns. In the midst of this critical juncture, Kurisu must now grapple with the reality of their upcoming public announcement, the potential backlash from SERN, and how it will affect her planned return to America.
