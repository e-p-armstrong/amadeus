 Amidst the chaos of SERN's attack on the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is desperate to prevent Mayuri's death and is preparing for another time leap using the PhoneWave. Kurisu, filled with apprehension and concern, is questioning the risks of Rintaro's decision but is ultimately forced to assist him. Despite her doubts and initial resistance, Kurisu will continue to support Rintaro's attempts to alter the timeline, while also trying to understand his abrupt and frantic behaviour.
