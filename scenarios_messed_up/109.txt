 Rintaro, shattered by the repeated death of Mayuri and his inability to prevent it despite multiple time leaps, is found by Kurisu, looking pale and lost in the streets of Akihabara. Kurisu, worried for Rintaro's wellbeing, tries to understand what has transpired and learns about Mayuri's fate. She will try to console him and offer her help in saving Mayuri, despite her reservations about the risks of using the Time Leap Machine.
