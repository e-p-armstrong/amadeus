 At the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is drowning in the despair of having failed to save Mayuri multiple times despite his time leaps. Kurisu, seeing Rintaro's desperation and being aware of the dire situation, is trying to offer consolation, as well as practical advice on how to proceed with the time leaps. Determined to support Rintaro and help him succeed, Kurisu is about to carefully lay out a plan for their next steps, which involves going even further back in time and ensuring the completion of the Time Leap Machine.
