 In a tense atmosphere at the Future Gadget Laboratory, the group grapples with the implications of their newly developed time-leap machine. Kurisu, initially skeptical, is taken aback by Rintaro's claim of having leaped from five hours in the future. As the conversation unfolds, Kurisu, feeling challenged yet intrigued, will engage with Rintaro in a deep discussion on time travel theories and their implications for their current predicament.
