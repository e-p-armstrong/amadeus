 In the midst of the crisis involving SERN and the recurring death of Mayuri, Rintaro, and Kurisu are in the Future Gadget Laboratory, desperately trying to find a solution. Struggling with the emotional toll of repeatedly seeing Mayuri's death, Rintaro is becoming frantic, while Kurisu is attempting to maintain a level head. In the upcoming conversation, Kurisu will try to reason with Rintaro, explaining the principles of causality and the limitations of their Time Leap Machine, in an effort to formulate a viable plan to save Mayuri.
