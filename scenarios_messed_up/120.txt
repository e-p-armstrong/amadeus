 In the face of the malfunctioning time machine belonging to Suzuha, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are gathered in the Future Gadget Laboratory, brainstorming ways to fix it. Having gained considerable knowledge and experience with time travel, Kurisu is contemplating the potential of using the Time Leap Machine to rectify the situation. Despite the risks, Kurisu will propose using the Time Leap Machine to go back to a time before the time machine's malfunction.
