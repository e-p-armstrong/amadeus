 In the midst of a tense atmosphere in the Future Gadget Lab, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are discussing their plan to repair the time machine, following Suzuha's revelation of her true identity as John Titor, Itaru's daughter from the future. Rintaro, desperate to save Mayuri from her repeated deaths, announces his plan to time leap again. Kurisu, though worried about the potential consequences of meddling with time, will question the efficacy of Rintaro's plan and brainstorm alternatives.
