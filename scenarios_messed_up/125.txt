 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu, Rintaro, and Suzuha are working together to repair Suzuha's time machine. Kurisu, feeling a sense of responsibility and determination, is focused on the task at hand, dismissing any interference from Rintaro and Daru. With her newfound knowledge about the lifter, Kurisu will dive back into the task, working diligently to repair the time machine and help Suzuha find her father.
