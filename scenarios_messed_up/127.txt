 After Suzuha has revealed her true identity as a time-traveler and entrusted Rintaro with an important mission, the group gathers in the Future Gadget Lab. Each member is processing the new information in their own way, with Itaru suggesting they make duplicates of the pin Suzuha gave to Rintaro to symbolize their unity. Kurisu, feeling a mix of shock and amusement, will make a sarcastic remark to Itaru, teasing him about his unusual thoughtfulness.
