 After a taxing chase through the streets of Akihabara, Kurisu and Itaru find themselves winded and out of breath, back at the Future Gadget Laboratory. Having just sent a D-mail that changed Suzuha's timeline, they are grappling with the consequences of their actions and the bizarre reality of their situation. Recovering from her exhaustion, Kurisu will engage Itaru in a philosophical conversation about the implications of time travel and its effects on personal relationships.
