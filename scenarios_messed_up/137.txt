 In the evening at the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro, Kurisu, and Mayuri have just finished a day of experimenting on the PhoneWave. As the team winds down, Rintaro unexpectedly addresses Kurisu by her name correctly, indicating a growing familiarity and concern for her well-being. Despite Rintaro's kind, protective gesture, Kurisu, caught off guard and slightly flustered, will insist on her independence, asserting that she can take care of herself.
