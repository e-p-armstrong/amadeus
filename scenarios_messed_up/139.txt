 In the aftermath of a sudden and violent raid on the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro is frantically trying to navigate the complexities of time leaping in a bid to save Mayuri. Kurisu, although initially surprised by Rintaro's plea for help, quickly slips into her role as a scientist to provide guidance and insights. Sensing the urgency and gravity of the situation, Kurisu will put her intellect to work, trying to unravel the mystery of the disappearing IBN 5100 and aiding Rintaro in his attempts to change the timeline.
