 Rintaro and Kurisu are in the Future Gadget Laboratory, reeling from the repeated failures to save Mayuri from her fated death. Kurisu, despite the tsundere exterior, is worried about Rintaro's well-being and is trying to convince him to use the Time Leap Machine. Amidst the tension and despair, Kurisu will argue her case, doing her best to support Rintaro and convince him to continue leaping.
