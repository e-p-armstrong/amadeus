 Rintaro and Kurisu find themselves in a dangerous situation, having attracted SERN's attention with their groundbreaking time travel experiments at the Future Gadget Laboratory. With SERN's agents hot on their heels, Rintaro and Kurisu are in a desperate bid to escape and bide time until their taxi arrives. Feeling anxious yet focused, Kurisu will rapidly devise a plan and guide Rintaro on how to use their surroundings to evade their pursuers.
