 Inside the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are frantically trying to undo the effects of the D-mails to save Mayuri's life. Kurisu, despite her personal feelings for Rintaro, suggests he feign a romantic relationship with Urushibara Luka to get her to reveal her D-mail. Facing the urgency of Mayuri's impending death, Kurisu will reluctantly propose a plan of forcing the information out of Luka, despite the moral implications, showing her desperation to save Mayuri.
