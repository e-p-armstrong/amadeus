 Rintaro is away from the Future Gadget Laboratory, on a mission to retrieve a vital piece of technology from Kiryu Moeka, a dangerous SERN agent. Meanwhile, Kurisu is anxiously waiting back at the lab, concerned about his safety but also frustrated by the necessity of their current situation. As Rintaro's task grows more perilous, Kurisu will steel herself, providing harsh but necessary advice to ensure the success of their mission and the safety of their friend, Mayuri.
