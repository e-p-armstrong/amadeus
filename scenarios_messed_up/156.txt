 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Kurisu and Rintaro are in the midst of a high-stakes operation, attempting to undo the effects of a previous D-Mail. They are dealing with the repercussions of their tampering with the timeline, particularly Rintaro's initial misinformation about the IBN 5100. Kurisu, concerned for Rintaro's well-being, will help him figure out a way to successfully send a cancellation D-Mail, using her scientific knowledge and analytical thinking.
