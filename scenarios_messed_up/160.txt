 At the peak of their time travel experiments, inside the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu are preparing to send a D-Mail with the hope of changing Moeka's fate. This is part of their desperate effort to undo the alterations in the timeline caused by their previous D-Mails. Kurisu, feeling conflicted and unsure, will provide Rintaro with her scientific perspective, despite wrestling with the moral implications of their actions.
