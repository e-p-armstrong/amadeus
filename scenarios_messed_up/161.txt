 In the heart of the series' conflict, Rintaro and Kurisu are battling against time and fate, attempting to undo the consequences of their D-mails to save Mayuri. They've just undone Moeka's D-mail, and faced a threat from Nae, which left them both shaken. A determined Kurisu, feeling an urgent need to fix things, will suggest another time leap to Rintaro, trying to keep him focused on the task at hand.
