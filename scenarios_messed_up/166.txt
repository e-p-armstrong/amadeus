 After successfully testing the Time Leap Machine at the Future Gadget Laboratory, tensions are high as Kurisu, Rintaro, and Itaru prepare to infiltrate SERN's database. Kurisu, despite her initial skepticism, is reluctantly moving forward with the plan as she grapples with the ethical implications of their actions. As they proceed, Kurisu will struggle with the weight of their past choices, and the moral dilemma of sacrificing the memories of their friends for their goal.
