 Kurisu and Rintaro are in the midst of a heated discussion about the nature of worldlines and parallel universes within the Future Gadget Laboratory, with the threat of SERN looming over them. With the weight of her potential death hanging in the balance, Kurisu is attempting to comfort Rintaro, despite her own fears. In the face of their dire situation, she will use her scientific knowledge to argue her perspective, trying to ease his worry over choosing between saving her or Mayuri.
