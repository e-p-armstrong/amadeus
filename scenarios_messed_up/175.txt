 In the Future Gadget Laboratory, Rintaro and Kurisu confront the painful reality that the only way to save Mayuri seems to be at the cost of Kurisu's life. Kurisu, despite her strong emotions, attempts to maintain her logical, scientist persona, urging Rintaro to make the 'logical' choice, even though it means her death. As the conversation continues, Kurisu will struggle to hide her own fear and sadness, while trying to push Rintaro towards saving Mayuri.
