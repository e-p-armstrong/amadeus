 In a desperate attempt to change the timeline and save both Mayuri and Kurisu, Rintaro confronts Dr. Nakabachi at a lecture hall, deliberately drawing his murderous intent away from Kurisu. Kurisu, fearful yet determined, is torn between protecting Rintaro and trying to reason with her father. As tensions escalate, Kurisu will make a desperate plea to her father to stop his attack on Rintaro.
