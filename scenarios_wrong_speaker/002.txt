
Kurisu, Okabe, and Itaru, a friend of Okabe's, have just stepped out of an elevator when Okabe unexpectedly bumps into Kurisu. To Okabe's shock, he recognizes Kurisu as someone he had seen dead just a few hours ago, leaving him confused and scared. Despite this being their first meeting, Kurisu finds herself tangled in Okabe's eccentric outburst and strange claims. Intrigued by Okabe's bizarre behavior and the mystery of the email that Itaru reveals, Kurisu is drawn into the situation, trying to make sense of the strange circumstances surrounding her unexpected encounter with Okabe.
